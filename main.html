Purpose

With this survey, we can easily determine how much students struggle with missing tasks during events like DSPC, ASMEPPS, division meets, readathons, and more. We can also identify how many tasks students accumulate during these events, analyze the impact of these missed tasks on their academic performance and overall stress levels, and pinpoint which subjects are often neglected during these events. By understanding these patterns, we can implement strategies to better support students in managing their workload and prioritizing their tasks effectively during busy periods.
